Issac Asimov: A Wise Guy (or a wiseguy?)

A wise man once said, "People who think they know everything are a great annoyance to those of us who do." His name was Isaac Asimov.

Asimov knew something about an awful lot of things, which is why he was a wise man.

However, there were also some things that Asimov knew nothing about. But he would not admit this to others.
