Looking for me?

I am an editor and project manager with more than 16 years experience. I’ve worked in print (newspaper & books), online, and academic publishing. I’m also currently cutting my teeth in front-end web development.

I live in Baltimore City with my wife, our two black cats, and more than 4,000 records. In my spare time, I enjoy chronicling the 1980s underground rock scene in a popular podcast; exploring the city of Baltimore and advocating on its behalf; occasionally DJ-ing in bars; watching the O's; and consuming as many flavors of ice cream as possible.
